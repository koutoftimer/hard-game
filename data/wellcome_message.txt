Welcome!
--------

This game suppose you are familiar with C programmming language or you are
willing to learn it. You've being warned!

Inspired by:

* Bitburner - a programming-based incremental game that revolves around hacking
  and cyberpunk themes.
* LostTerminal - A hard sci-fi hopepunk podcast following the journey of a
  little satellite trying to understand what has happened after Earth stops
  returning his calls.
* PirateSoftware - founder of Pirate Software game studio and host of the game
  jam with the theme: "you are a weapon".
